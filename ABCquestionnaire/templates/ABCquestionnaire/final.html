  • The 17 questions you filled in were intended to try and describe dimensions
    of Academic Behavioural Confidence (ABC) (related to the self-concept and
    self-efficacy of students)
  • The identified four dimensions of ABC, are, Grades("Confidence in the actual
    activity contributing directly to what a student is graded on, including
    coursework and exams"), Verbalising ("Confidence in the ability to verbalise
    their knowledge during their learning activity"), Attendance ("Confidence in
    the willingness to appropriately attend events during their learning activity")
    and Studying ("Confidence in undertaking the self-orginised activity
    necessary for their learning experience").
